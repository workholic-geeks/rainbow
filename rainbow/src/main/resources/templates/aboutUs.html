About US

Rainbow Capital Ltd is a micro lending company promoted by a group of investors with considerable experience in variety of business activities in Zambia.The start up is an answer to the growing needs of expanding aspiration of people of Zambia.The Company is headed by a very senior banker and supported by experienced operational and marketing team.The Company aims to provides flexible loans with a maximum tenor of 24 months for every possible need on a fully digitalised and transparent platform.With a world class technology platform the company intends to be a game changer in the micro lending business.
